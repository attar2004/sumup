🧠 Smart Maths Tricks

⚡ Multiply by 5

Step 1: Multiply number by 10

Step 2: Divide by 2

Example: 24 × 5 = 120

⚡ Square Ending in 5

Step 1: Multiply first digit with next number

Step 2: Add 25 at end

Example: 25² = 625

⚡ Multiply by 9

Multiply by 10 and subtract the number

Example: 23 × 9 = (23×10) - 23 = 230 - 23 = 207

⚡ Add Fast

Round numbers first

Example: 48 + 29 → (50 + 29) - 2 = 77
