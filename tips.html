🧩 Break Problems Smartly

Step 1: Round numbers (48 → 50)

Step 2: Solve easier version

Step 3: Adjust answer

Example:
48 + 29 → (50 + 29) - 2 = 77

Examples

Addition: 48 + 29 → (50 + 29) - 2 = 77

Multiplication: 12 × 19 → (12 × 20) - 12 = 240 - 12 = 228

Subtraction: 100 - 47 → 53

Division: 84 ÷ 4 → 80 ÷ 4 = 20, 4 ÷ 4 = 1 → 21
